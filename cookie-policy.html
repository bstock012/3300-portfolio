What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit, which can improve your browsing experience.

Cookies used on this website

This website uses Google Analytics to track anonymized usage data. The cookies used by Google Analytics allow us to:

  • Count visits and traffic sources
  • Understand which pages are most popular
  • Improve website functionality and performance

These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information. For more details on how Google Analytics handles data, you can visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

Managing cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies at any time using your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website.

Third-party cookies

This website does not use any third-party cookies except those set by Google Analytics for anonymized site usage tracking.
