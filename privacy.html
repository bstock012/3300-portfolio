Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and protected when you visit this website. This portfolio website is operated by Blaine Stockman.

Information I Collect

This website does not collect any personal information directly from users. However, it does use Google Analytics to gather basic, non-personal usage data.

Use of Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a web analytics service provided by Google. It collects anonymized, non-personally identifiable information such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on each page
  • Browser type and device information
  • General geographic location (non-precise)

Google Analytics uses cookies to track this information. You can learn more about how Google uses data by visiting: “How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.”

Cookies

This site uses cookies only for the purpose of Google Analytics. These cookies do not allow me to personally identify you. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer not to be tracked.

Third-Party Disclosure

I do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Google Analytics data is handled according to Google's privacy policies.

Data Protection

Since no personal data is collected directly by this website, no personal data is stored on my servers. Google Analytics processes anonymized usage data securely on Google’s systems.

Links to External Sites

This website may contain links to external websites such as LinkedIn, GitHub, or Instagram. Clicking on those links may take you to websites that have their own privacy policies. I am not responsible for the content or practices of external sites.

Children’s Privacy

This website is not directed toward children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Your Consent

By using this website, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
